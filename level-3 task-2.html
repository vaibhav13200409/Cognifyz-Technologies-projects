About the Internship

Join us for an exciting journey into web development! Our internship program offers you the opportunity to work on real-world projects, gain industry-relevant experience, and learn from experienced mentors. This program spans 1 month and provides hands-on experience in developing web applications.

Internship Highlights

  • Work on live projects with real-world impact.
  • Receive mentorship from experienced web developers.
  • Build a portfolio to showcase your skills.
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals.
  • Certificate of completion to boost your career prospects.

Qualifications

We are looking for passionate individuals with the following skills and interests:

  • Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks like React or Angular (optional).
  • Eagerness to learn and adapt to new technologies.
  • Problem-solving skills and a collaborative attitude.
