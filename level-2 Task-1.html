India Gate

This is a brief description for India Gate. At the centre of New Delhi stands the 42 m high India Gate, an "Arc-de-Triomphe" like archway in the middle of a crossroad. Almost similar to its French counterpart, it commemorates the 70,000 Indian soldiers who lost their lives fighting for the British Army during the World War I.

This is a brief description for Shree Jagannatha Temple Puri. The Jagannath Temple is made so that it casts no shadow here on earth at any time of day. The temple's 20-foot-high chakra is positioned such that the entire chakra can be seen from everywhere in the city.
